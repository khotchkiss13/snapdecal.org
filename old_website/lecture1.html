Lecture 1: Welcome to Snap! Below the Line!

Welcome to Snap! Below the Line, Spring 2015! We are very excited to teach you how to become a Snap! Developer. On this website, we will be providing all of the information that you need for this course! When you click on a lecture topic, you will be brought to a page like this, that will include a brief summary of the day's lesson, and a link to all resources that will be used in class. Each leacture page will open at approximately 11:59 P.M. on the Tuesday before the class, allowing for all students to be thoroughly prepared!

Today's class will focus on introducing you to everything that you need to know in order to succeed in this class.
