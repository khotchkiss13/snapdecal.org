Lecture 9: Project Work

Today's class is for you to continue working on your project. Make sure you have a solid proposal approved by us before you begin your work. Also have your README file created on GitHub detailing the changes/functionality of your project and the steps you will take to complete it. We will be checking off your progress during class next week to verify that you are on the right track.
