Lecture 7: Making Changes to Snap!: Advanced

In today's class, we will be discussing how to make more complex and drastic changes to Snap!, and you will complete the resolution of a particular issue. Make sure to successfully complete and update, if needed, your pull request for your chosen issue during the upcoming week.

This lecture is very important as it is the foundation to your final project!
